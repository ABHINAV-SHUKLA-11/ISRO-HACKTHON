6 Novel Contributions

All six are unpublished for LISS-IV specifically — this is the core differentiator for BAH 2026 judges.
01

Prithvi-EO-2.0 Adaptation

First use of this foundation model for LISS-IV cloud removal globally.

02

3-Band Adapter

LISS-IV has no SWIR — solved via a learnable 3→6 band projection.

03

NER Monsoon Augmentation

Cloud generator tuned to North-East India's specific optical depth profiles.

04

Dual-Path Routing

Thin and thick cloud pixels take different reconstruction paths.

05

NDVI-Constrained Loss

Spectral fidelity for agriculture enforced directly in the training objective.

06

Uncertainty Map Output

MC Dropout gives a per-pixel confidence score for downstream trust.
